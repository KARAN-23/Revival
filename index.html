Introduction and Company Description

Revive is a pioneering beauty-tech startup aiming to revolutionize skincare in India. Our AI-powered Smart Mirror scans your skin, tracks pollution, and delivers personalized ingredient-based skincare routines. We blend AI, environmental data science, and wellness to address the unique skin health challenges of Indian consumers.

Mission Statement

To empower every individual with precise, data-backed skincare guidance using technology and environmental insights.

Problem–Solution Fit

  • Consumers face trial-and-error with generic skincare products.
  • Revive offers daily skin analysis, weather-aware advice, and tailored routines for Indian climates and lifestyles.
